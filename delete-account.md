# Delete Your Intelli Notes Account

We respect your privacy and give you full control over your data.

## How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your Intelli Notes account and associated data, follow these steps:

1. Send an email to: **karanmotghare8@gmail.com**
2. Use the subject line: **Delete My Intelli Notes Account**
3. In the email, include:
    - The email address used to sign in to Intelli Notes

We will verify your request and process it as soon as possible.

## What data will be deleted

Once your request is processed, the following data will be permanently deleted:

- Your Intelli Notes user account
- All notes created by you
- Synced note data stored in our database

## Data retention

- No personal data is retained after account deletion.
- Backup or log data (if any) is automatically removed within **30 days**.
