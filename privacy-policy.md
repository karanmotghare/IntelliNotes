# Privacy Policy for Intelli Notes

Last updated: 6 February 2026

Intelli Notes ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Intelli Notes mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Email address (used for authentication and account identification)

### User-Generated Content
- Notes created by you within the app

### Device & Usage Information
- Basic device information (for app functionality and debugging)
- Crash logs and diagnostics (if enabled)

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Authenticate users
- Sync notes securely across devices
- Improve app performance and stability
- Diagnose crashes and technical issues

---

## Data Storage & Security

- Notes are stored locally on your device.
- If cloud sync is enabled, notes are securely transmitted and stored using encrypted connections (HTTPS).
- We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

---

## Data Sharing

We do **not** share your personal data with:
- Advertisers
- Analytics companies
- Third-party marketers

Your data is used **only** to provide and improve the Intelli Notes app experience.

## Data Deletion

You may delete your notes at any time from within the app.
